Our Objective

Our goal is to assist developing natives' conversational skills in English by offering the opportunity to work with heritage English speakers in an intensive two-week program. We aim to boost our students' confidence in speaking, no matter what level they may be at, as well as equip our students with the conversational English they can take into the professional realm - to job interviews, building connections, and much more. Notably, our conversational platform is what sets us apart from other programs. While most English institutes and schools abroad focus upon reading and writing, speaking is the most difficult aspect of a language, as it requires both the synthesis of and production of new content of the language. As a result, we believe that improving students' speaking abilities will ultimately improve their English abilities the most.
