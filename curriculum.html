What Sets Us Apart

We believe in our conversational platform and curriculum. While other English programs may focus on reading and writing, we make sure to cover speaking skills because speaking is the most difficult aspect of a language. It requires the real-time synthesis, production, and recollection of language. Students improve their English most when they learn to speak.

Our program contains 3 major sections designed to increase ease of speaking. Students will work on a final project to be evaluated by both teachers and peers.
