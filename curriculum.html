What Sets Us Apart

What sets us apart is our conversational platform and curriculum. While other English programs may focus on reading and writing, we make sure to emphasize speaking.

Speaking is by far the most difficult aspect of a language. It requires the real-time synthesis, production, and recollection of language. People therefore improve their English most when they learn to speak.

Our program contains three major sections designed to increase ease of speaking. Students will work on a final project to be evaluated by both teachers and peers.
