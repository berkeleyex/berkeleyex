Our Curriculum

This intensive two-week program will be divided into three rounds of three days: Who We Are, What We Do, How We Thrive. Each round will begin by grounding itself on shared media (article, audio clip, video) and by understanding and introducing the context and language related to that topic. The following days will incorporate activities specifically designed to increase student inquiry and ease of speaking to other English speakers, including native speakers. The final day will be a presentation or project thatwill be evaluated by both the teachers and surrounding peers.

Students will also receive more personalized instruction with 25-minute small group sessions that specifically target and develop proficiency and fluency in spoken conversation.

Class Structure

  • COURSE LENGTH: 2 weeks
  • TIMETABLE: Monday-Friday
  • ENTRY LEVEL: Beginner to Advanced
  • COURSE STRUCTURE: 60 minutes of class instruction, 25 minutes of small group sessions
